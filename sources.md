## Notes for Verification

### What to Check:
1. **Language**: Verify if they have English sections or RSS feeds
2. **Update Frequency**: Check how often they publish new content
3. **Content Quality**: Assess the type of local news they cover
4. **RSS/API Access**: Look for RSS feeds or API endpoints for content aggregation
5. **Copyright Policy**: Check their terms for content usage and attribution requirements
